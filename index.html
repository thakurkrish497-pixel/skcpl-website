ABOUT SKCPL

Total Turnkey Solutions

We, S.Kumar Creation Pvt. Ltd., take the opportunity to introduce ourselves as a total turnkey solution provider with over 16 years of experience in India across Telecom, Infrastructure, Real Estate, & Hospitality sectors.

SKCPL is the government-appointed accreditation body of INDIA responsible for providing accreditation of conformity assessment bodies (CABs) in the fields of certification and inspection. We are registered with MPPWD, MPEB BHOPAL, A class electrical contractor registered with MSME & NSIC, and registered with OHSAS.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to contribute to the country's development by providing the best Telecom, Infrastructure, & Real Estate company. Our culture is based on openness and respect, empowering our team members to play a significant role in achieving our collective goals.
