ABOUT SKCPL

Total Turnkey Solution Provider

We, S. Kumar Creation Pvt. Ltd., take the opportunity to introduce ourselves as a total turnkey solution provider since last 16 years in India in Telecom, Infrastructure, Real Estate & Hospitality.

We are registered with MPPWD, MPEB Bhopal, A class electrical contractor registered with MSME & NSIC, and registered with OHSAS. Our team of seasoned professionals, including architects, engineers and marketing specialists, deliver what they promise.

Our Mission is to contribute to the country's development by providing the best Telecom, Infrastructure & Real Estate services. Our company culture is based on openness and respect, empowering our team members to play a significant role in achieving individual and group goals.
