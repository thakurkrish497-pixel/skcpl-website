Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers about S. Kumar Creation, our services, resort bookings, and solar solutions.

What services does S. Kumar Creation Pvt. Ltd. (SKCPL) provide?

keyboard_arrow_down

SKCPL is a total turnkey solution provider. For the last 16 years, we have provided premium services in Telecom Projects (BTS installation, RF survey, commissioning), Infrastructure development (roads, transmission lines, urban utilities), Real Estate (construction, architecture, renovation), and Hospitality.

Is S. Kumar Creation registered with government departments?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, we are registered with MPPWD (MP Public Works Department), MPEB Bhopal (A-class electrical contractor), MSME, NSIC, and certified under OHSAS. This permits us to execute government and public infrastructure projects of any scale.

What activities are available at Fun N Food Resort, Indore?

keyboard_arrow_down

Fun N Food Family Restaurant Resort & Water Park offers a wide range of attractions including a beautiful swimming pool, a high-energy rain dance area, cricket/football grounds, indoor games (snooker, billiards, carom, chess), a multi-cuisine restaurant, and beautiful marriage gardens for events.

Where is the resort located and how do I book?

keyboard_arrow_down

The resort is located on Khandwa Road, near BSNL Exchange, Tejaji Nagar, Indore. For bookings, marriages, corporate outings, or general enquiries, you can fill out our online form on fun-n-food.html or call 9479800333.

Does SKumar Solar provide government subsidies for solar rooftops?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, we provide end-to-end support under current central government subsidy schemes, such as the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana. We handle documentation, grid connection approval, and net metering liaisoning with Madhya Pradesh West Zone (MPWZ) electricity board.

What solar equipment manufacturers do you partner with?

keyboard_arrow_down

We are authorized distributors and system integrators with top-tier solar manufacturers including Adani Solar, Havells, Luminous, Waaree, Microtek, and KStar. This allows us to supply systems that feature performance guarantees up to 25 years.
