ABOUT US

Your Perfect Getaway

Located near Tejaji Nagar, Khandwa Road, Fun & Food Resort N Water Park is Indore's premier destination for leisure, celebrations, and corporate events.

As a proud member of the SKCPL Group, we bring over a decade of hospitality excellence to every guest. Whether you're looking for a thrilling day at the water park, a luxurious stay, or the perfect venue for your dream wedding, our resort offers world-class amenities nestled in a serene environment.
