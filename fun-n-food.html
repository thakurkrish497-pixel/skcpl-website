ABOUT US

Your Perfect Getaway in Indore

Located near BSNL Exchange, Tejaji Nagar on Khandwa Road, Fun N Food Family Restaurant Resort & Water Park is Indore's premier destination for leisure, celebrations, and corporate events.

As a proud member of the SKCPL Group, we bring over a decade of hospitality excellence to every guest. Whether you're looking for a thrilling water park experience, a luxurious venue for your dream wedding, or an exciting family day out — Fun N Food has it all.

Fun N Food offers Indian families fun-filled and affordable vacations — featuring a beautiful swimming pool, rain dance area, cricket and football grounds, badminton court, indoor games (snooker, billiards, carom, chess), spacious marriage garden, and a multi-cuisine restaurant.
